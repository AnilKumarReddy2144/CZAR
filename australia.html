Australia Visa Types
    Australia Tourist Visa for Indians (Subclass 600) – the Australia Tourist Visa for Indians Allows You to Visit Australia as a Visitor/tourist to Meet Family and Friends or for Enjoy Your Vacations, Wherein the Maximum Stay Period Can Be of 3 Months or as Per the Australia Tourist Visa.

    Australia Business Visa for Indians – the Australia Business Visa for Indians Allows You to travel to Australia for Business Purpose Such as Attending the Business Meetings, Conferences, Seminars, and Training and the Stay Period with This Visa Is a Maximum of 90 Days or as Per the Australia Business Visa.

    Australia Transit Visa for Indians (Subclass 771) – the Australia Transit Visa Allows You to Transit Through Australia While Traveling to Another Country, but the Stay Must Be No Longer Than 72 Hours. Australia Transit Visa May Allow Only a Single Entry or as Per the Ticket.

    Australia Student Visa for Indians (Subclass 500) – the Australia Visa for Indian Students Allows You to Study in Australia Wherein the Age of the Student Must Be More Than 6 Years. They Can Stay in Australia for Up to 5 Years or as Per the Course Enrolled.

    Entertainment Activities (Subclass 408) – This Visa Lets You Work Temporarily in Australia. the Entertainment Industry, Film, Television, Performer or Production Staff Can Apply for This Visa and It Allows You to Stay in Australia for Up to 2 Years.
Document requirements for Australia visa from India in 2023
Passport: A valid passport with at least six months of validity from entry to Australia. Make sure that your passport has at least two Blank pages.

Visa Application Form: The online Australia visa application form needs to be completed directly and submitted along with the needed documents.

Covering Letter: A covering letter addressing to Australian Embassy.

Photos: You must give one recent Passport-Size color photo with a White Background.

Trip Itinerary: A detailed trip itinerary including travel plans in Australia.

Financial Proof: You need to give evidence of sufficient finances to cover your trip charges in Australia. This could include bank statements, Three Years Income Tax Returns, Fixed Deposits, Income Tax Returns, any property papers if you own the same.

Employment Proof: Give employment evidence, Three Months’ Salary slips, leave letter or Business license If you’re Self Employed.

Health and Character Conditions: You can be asked by the authorities to to give Health documents. If you’re over the age of 70 or Intend to stay in Australia for more than 3 months.

Travel Insurance: It’s recommended to have travel Insurance that covers medical charges, hospitalisation in case of any difficulty During your stay in Australia.

Frequently Asked Questions

Top FAQs Asked by students travelling to Australia

1. What is the Processing Time for Australia visa from India in 2023?

The Processing time for Australia Visa from India varies depending on various factors, such as the complexity of your case, the completeness of your application, and the volume of applications received by the Australian High Commission. The processing time for Australia visa is about 18 – 20 days as of 2023. However, some Australia Visa applications may take longer or shorter depending on individual circumstances. You can check the Australia Visa status of your application online through your ImmiAccount or by using the Visa Entitlement Verification Online (VEVO) service.

2. What if your Australian Visa is Rejected?

If your Australia Visa in India is rejected then you receive a letter through the mail explaining the reasons for the rejection of Australia Visa. You can still apply for Australia Visa provided you are able to justify the reasons mentioned on your rejection letter or till your circumstances improve from the reasons mentioned on the rejection letter.
